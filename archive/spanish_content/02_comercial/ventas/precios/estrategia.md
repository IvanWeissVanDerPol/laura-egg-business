## 6. Dynamic Pricing

### Seasonal Adjustments

| Period | Price Adjustment | Reason |
|--------|-----------------|--------|
| January-March | -5% | Post-holiday, lower demand |
| April-June | Base | Standard pricing |
| July-September | +5% | Winter, higher consumption |
| October-December | +10% | Holiday baking season |

### Market Conditions

| Situation | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Competitor price drop | Match only if margin allows |
| Demand surge | Raise 5-10%, limit orders |
| Supply shortage | Raise 15-20% |
| New competitor | Hold price, emphasize quality |
