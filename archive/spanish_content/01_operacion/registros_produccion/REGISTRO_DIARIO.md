### Daily Checklist

#### Morning (6:00 AM - 8:00 AM)
- [ ] Check water systems (nipples, drinkers, tanks)
- [ ] Inspect bird behavior and health
- [ ] Check feed bins and feeders
- [ ] Record temperature and humidity
- [ ] Collect first round of eggs

#### Midday (12:00 PM - 1:00 PM)
- [ ] Monitor feed consumption
- [ ] Check water consumption
- [ ] Observe flock behavior
- [ ] Remove dead birds (if any)
- [ ] Second egg collection

#### Afternoon (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM)
- [ ] Final egg collection
- [ ] Clean nesting areas
- [ ] Check egg quality (sample)
- [ ] Record daily mortality
- [ ] Secure facility
