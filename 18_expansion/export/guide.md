## 2. Export Requirements

### SENACSA Requirements

**Registration:**
- [ ] SENACSA export registration
- [ ] Registered establishment
- [ ] HACCP certification
- [ ] Traceability system

**Documentation:**
- [ ] Sanitary certificate
- [ ] Health certificate
- [ ] Origin certificate
- [ ] Commercial invoice
- [ ] Packing list

**Facility Requirements:**
- [ ] Approved establishment
- [ ] Inspection-ready facilities
- [ ] Cold chain capability
- [ ] Proper labeling in Spanish

### SENACSA Export Process

1. **Registration**: Apply as exporter
2. **Facility Inspection**: SENACSA approves premises
3. **Product Inspection**: Batch-by-batch approval
4. **Documentation**: Issue certificates
5. **Customs**: Clear with documentation

### Timeline
- Initial registration: 2-3 months
- First shipment: +1-2 months
- Ongoing: Per shipment

## 3. Market Analysis

### Argentina Market

**Size:**
- Population: 45 million
- Annual consumption: ~300,000 tons eggs
- Imports: Minimal (mostly self-sufficient)

**Opportunities:**
- Border regions (Clorinda, Posadas proximity)
- Premium segment (organic, free-range)
- Processed products

**Challenges:**
- Currency restrictions
- Strong local competition
- Complex regulations

**Price Potential:**
- Table eggs: $1.80-2.20 USD/kg
- Premium: $2.50-3.00 USD/kg

### Brazil Market

**Size:**
- Population: 200+ million
- World's 3rd largest producer
- Mostly self-sufficient

**Opportunities:**
- Border region trade (Paraguay side)
- Niche premium products
- Processed eggs

**Challenges:**
- Very competitive market
- Brazilian real valuation
- Large domestic production

**Price Potential:**
- Similar to Paraguay prices
- Premium opportunity limited

## 5. Export Pricing

|------|-----------------|-------|

## 6. Logistics

### Route Options

**To Argentina (Buenos Aires):**
```
Farm → Encarnación → Posadas bridge → Buenos Aires
Distance: ~1,200 km
Transport: Refrigerated truck
Time: 18-24 hours
Cost: $0.15-0.20/kg
```

**To Brazil (São Paulo):**
```
Farm → Ciudad del Este → Foz do Iguaçu → São Paulo
Distance: ~900 km
Transport: Refrigerated truck
Time: 12-16 hours
Cost: $0.12-0.18/kg
```

### Cold Chain for Export

| Stage | Temperature | Duration |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Farm cooler | 8-12°C | 0-24 hours |
| Transport | 8-12°C | 12-24 hours |
| Border wait | 8-12°C | 2-6 hours |
| International | 8-12°C | 12-24 hours |

**Critical:** Product must arrive at <12°C

## 8. Payment Terms

### Standard Export Terms

| Term | Description | Risk |
|------|-------------|------|
| CAD (Cash against docs) | Pay before release | Low |
| LC (Letter of Credit) | Bank guaranteed | Very low |
| TT (Wire transfer) | Advance payment | Low |
| Open account | Pay after delivery | Higher |

### Recommended Terms
- First order: CAD or advance
- Established relationship: LC or 30 days
- Never ship without payment assurance

### Currency
- USD preferred
- Lock exchange rate in contract
- Factor in currency fluctuation risk
