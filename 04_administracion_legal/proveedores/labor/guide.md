# Labor Management Guide

## 1. Workforce Requirements

### Staffing by Farm Size

| Flock Size | Workers | Roles |
|------------|---------|-------|
| 500 hens | 1-2 | Owner + part-time |
| 1,000 hens | 2-3 | Owner + 1-2 workers |
| 3,000 hens | 3-5 | Manager + workers |
| 5,000 hens | 5-7 | Manager + specialists |

### Daily Time Requirements

| Task | Time (hrs) | Frequency |
|------|------------|-----------|
| Feeding | 1-2 | Daily |
| Egg collection | 2-3 | 2-3x daily |
| Cleaning | 1-2 | Daily |
| Monitoring | 1 | Daily |
| Maintenance | 2 | Weekly |
| Delivery | 2-4 | 2-3x weekly |

## 2. Job Descriptions

### 2.1 Farm Worker (Operario)

**Responsibilities:**
- Daily feeding and watering
- Egg collection and handling
- Cleaning and sanitation
- Bird health monitoring
- Basic record keeping
- Facility maintenance

**Requirements:**
- Basic education
- Experience with animals preferred
- Honest and reliable
- Physical ability

**Compensation:**
- Minimum wage: Gs. 2,500,000/month
- Plus: Overtime, bonuses, food

### 2.2 Farm Manager

**Responsibilities:**
- Oversee all farm operations
- Production planning
- Quality control
- Staff supervision
- Record analysis
- Supplier relations
- Sales coordination

**Requirements:**
- Secondary education minimum
- Agricultural experience
- Basic accounting
- Leadership skills

**Compensation:**
- Salary: Gs. 4,000,000 - 7,000,000/month
- Plus: Performance bonuses

### 2.3 Delivery Driver

**Responsibilities:**
- Transport eggs to customers
- Collect payments
- Customer relations
- Vehicle maintenance
- Route optimization

**Requirements:**
- Driver's license
- Vehicle experience
- Customer service skills

**Compensation:**
- Salary: Gs. 3,000,000 - 4,500,000/month
- Plus: Fuel, delivery bonuses

## 3. Recruitment

### Where to Find Workers

| Source | Best For | Cost |
|--------|----------|------|
| Neighbors/Community | Entry level | Free |
| Facebook groups | Various | Free |
| Centro Ideas/MTCP | Trained workers | Free |
| Word of mouth | Experienced | Free |
| Employment agencies | Specialized | Gs. 200,000+ |

### Interview Questions

1. Have you worked with poultry before?
2. Can you work early mornings?
3. Are you comfortable with animals?
4. Do you have your own transportation?
5. What are your salary expectations?
6. Can you work weekends if needed?

### Selection Criteria
- [ ] Experience (preferable, not required)
- [ ] References from previous employers
- [ ] Punctuality (arrive on time)
- [ ] Honesty (critical for farm access)
- [ ] Physical capability
- [ ] Willingness to learn

## 4. Employment Types

### Permanent Full-Time
- 8 hours/day, 6 days/week
- All benefits
- Gs. 2,500,000 - 4,000,000/month

### Permanent Part-Time
- 4-6 hours/day
- Proportional benefits
- Gs. 1,500,000 - 2,500,000/month

### Temporary/Seasonal
- For specific projects
- Daily or weekly pay
- No permanent benefits

### Family Labor
- Common for small farms
- Informal arrangement
- Still track hours/value

## 5. Labor Laws (Paraguay)

### Requirements
- Written contract
- Minimum wage compliance
- Social security (IPS) contributions
- Annual leave (14 days after 1 year)
- Sunday rest or premium pay
- Overtime at 50% premium

### IPS Contributions
| Type | Rate |
|------|------|
| Employer | 16.5% |
| Employee | 9% |

### Mandatory Benefits
- [ ] 13th salary (aguinaldo) - December
- [ ] Vacation leave (after 1 year)
- [ ] IPS health insurance
- [ ] Overtime pay
- [ ] Safe working conditions

## 8. Worker Motivation

### Non-Monetary Benefits
- Regular schedule
- Sundays off
- Fresh eggs (Gs. 50,000 value/month)
- Lunch provided
- Safe working environment
- Recognition

### Incentive Programs

**Production Bonus:**
- Gs. 50,000 bonus if monthly production exceeds target
- Per worker

**Quality Bonus:**
- Gs. 25,000 if rejects <3%
- Monthly

**Attendance Bonus:**
- Gs. 100,000 for perfect month
- No absences, no tardiness

**Longevity Bonus:**
- Gs. 200,000 after 1 year
- Additional Gs. 100,000 each year
