## 2. RUC (Registro Único del Contribuyente)

### What It Is
Tax identification number for all businesses in Paraguay.

### Where to Get
SET (Subsecretaría de Estado de Tributación)
- Central office in Asunción
- Online registration available

### Requirements
- [ ] Completed form
- [ ] Photo ID (CEDULA)
- [ ] Proof of address
- [ ] Business activity description
- [ ] Initial capital declaration

### Timeline
- 3-5 business days

### Cost
- Free

### Business Activities to Register
- Egg production (avicultura)
- Poultry farming
- Agricultural product sales

## 3. Municipal Operating License

### Where to Get
Your local municipality (Luque, Areguá, etc.)

### Requirements
- [ ] RUC certificate
- [ ] Property documents (title or lease)
- [ ] Business plan summary
- [ ] Site plan
- [ ] Environmental self-assessment
- [ ] SENACSA registration

### Costs
| Item | Cost (Gs.) |
|------|------------|
| Patent registration | 100,000 - 500,000 |
| Annual license | 200,000 - 800,000 |
| Variable by municipality | |

### Timeline
- 2-4 weeks

## 4. SENACSA Registration

### What It Is
Registration with Servicio Nacional de Calidad y Salud Animal for poultry producers.

### Where to Get
SENACSA offices - Central or regional

### Requirements
- [ ] Application form
- [ ] Property documents
- [ ] Farm plan
- [ ] Vaccination program
- [ ] Biosecurity plan
- [ ] Species registration
- [ ] Production capacity declaration

### Costs
- Registration: Gs. 200,000 - 500,000
- Annual renewal: Gs. 100,000 - 200,000

### Timeline
- 2-4 weeks for initial
- Annual renewal

### Benefits
- Official producer status
- Required for institutional sales
- Access to government programs
- Animal health support

## 5. Environmental Permit (AUI/AU)

### What It Is
Environmental Authorization from MADES (Ministerio del Ambiente y Desarrollo Sostenible)

### When Required
- Commercial production (>500 birds)
- Near water sources
- Residential areas nearby

### Categories

| Category | Requirements | Cost |
|----------|--------------|------|
| Declaración Jurada | Self-assessment | Free |
| AUI Simplificado | Basic review | Gs. 500,000 |
| AUI Completo | Full EIA | Gs. 2,000,000+ |

### Application Process
1. Complete environmental self-assessment
2. Submit to MADES
3. Site visit (may be required)
4. Approval or request more info
5. Receive authorization

### Timeline
- 30-90 days

### Environmental Requirements
- [ ] Waste management plan
- [ ] Manure handling
- [ ] Water protection
- [ ] Buffer zones
- [ ] Emission controls

## 6. Water Use Permit (ANA)

### When Required
- Deep well installation
- Large water withdrawals
- Near rivers/streams

### Where to Get
ANA (Autoridad del Agua)
- Environmental authority

### Requirements
- [ ] Application form
- [ ] Hydrological study
- [ ] Water testing
- [ ] Impact assessment
- [ ] Usage projections

### Timeline
- 60-120 days

## 7. Building Permits

### When Required
- New construction
- Major renovations
- Changing structure

### Where to Get
Municipalidad de [your area]

### Requirements
- [ ] Architectural plans
- [ ] Structural calculations
- [ ] Site plan
- [ ] Setbacks verified
- [ ] Neighbor notifications

### Costs
- By construction value
- Typically 1-2% of construction cost

### Timeline
- 30-60 days

## 8. Fire Safety Certificate

### What It Is
Certification from BOMBEROS that facility meets fire safety standards.

### Where to Get
Bomberos Voluntarios de Paraguay

### Requirements
- [ ] Application
- [ ] Site inspection
- [ ] Fire extinguisher placement
- [ ] Emergency exits
- [ ] Electrical safety
- [ ] Access for emergency vehicles

### Costs
- Inspection: Gs. 100,000 - 300,000
- Certificate: Gs. 50,000 - 100,000

### Timeline
- 1-2 weeks after inspection

## 11. Common Issues

### Delays and Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Missing documents | Prepare complete package |
| Site visit delays | Schedule proactively |
| Missing signatures | Get all required signatories |
| Wrong forms | Verify with authority |
| Fee disputes | Escalate to supervisor |

### Pro Tips
1. **Visit offices in person** - Faster than mail
2. **Build relationships** - Same officers help
3. **Get everything in writing** - Verbal promises don't count
4. **Start early** - Permits take time
5. **Use a gestor** - Administrative helper for permits
