## 📝 TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Equipment Needed:
- **Camera:** DSLR or mirrorless (full-frame preferred)
- **Lenses:** 
  - 24-70mm f/2.8 (versatile workhorse)
  - 85mm f/1.8 or 50mm f/1.4 (portraits)
  - 100mm macro (detail shots)
  - Wide angle 16-35mm (establishing shots)
- **Lighting:** 
  - Natural light (preferred)
  - Reflector for fill
  - Softbox/LED panel for product shots if needed
- **Tripod:** For sharp images and consistency
- **Props:** 
  - Wooden boards/surfaces
  - Linens (natural colors)
  - Rustic baskets
  - Kitchen utensils (wood, vintage style)

### Image Requirements:
- **Resolution:** Minimum 2400x1600 pixels (print quality)
- **Format:** RAW + JPEG (deliver both)
- **Color Profile:** sRGB for web
- **File Naming:** Organized by category (e.g., "01_Hero_Laura_001.RAW")

### Lighting Guidelines:
- **Golden Hour:** All hero shots, portraits, farm establishing shots
- **Soft Natural Light:** Product photography
- **Avoid:** Harsh midday sun (unless for specific effect)
- **Indoor:** Near windows with diffused light

### Styling Notes:
- **Color Palette:** Warm earth tones, natural materials
- **Avoid:** Plastic, artificial elements
- **Props:** Wood, straw, burlap, ceramic, linen
- **Mood:** Authentic, rustic, premium but approachable

## 💡 PRO TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

### Getting the Best Shots:
1. **Shoot during golden hour** whenever possible for warm, inviting images
2. **Bring a helper** to assist with chickens, props, and lighting
3. **Take multiple variations** of each shot (lighting changes, expressions)
4. **Capture candid moments** between posed shots - often the best
5. **Show the yolks:** The bright orange yolks are a key selling point
6. **Include context:** Wide shots that show scale and environment
7. **Focus on eyes:** In portraits, sharp eyes are critical
8. **Clean backgrounds:** Remove distracting elements before shooting

### Communication with Laura:
- Explain each shot before taking it
- Show preview images when possible
- Ask for her input on best angles/features
- Make it fun and relaxed for natural expressions
