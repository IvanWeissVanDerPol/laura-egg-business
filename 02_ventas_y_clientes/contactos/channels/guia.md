## 2. Channel Descriptions

### 2.1 Supermarkets & Retail Chains

**Potential Partners:**
- Stock (Cadiex group)
- Casa Rica
- El Dorado
- Super 6
- Libre
- Ykua

**Order Characteristics**
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Order Frequency | 2-3x weekly |
| Minimum Order | 30-50 dozen |
| Payment Terms | 15-30 days |
| Required | Barcode, consistent quality |

**Requirements**
- [ ] Product barcode registration
- [ ] Consistent daily/weekly volume
- [ ] Refrigerated display
- [ ] Regular delivery schedule
- [ ] Invoicing (facturas)
- [ ] Marketing support

**Pros:**
- Large volume potential
- Brand visibility
- Regular orders

**Cons:**
- Low margins (5-10%)
- Strict quality requirements
- Payment delays
- Heavy paperwork

### 2.2 Restaurants & Hotels

**Target Segments**
- Route 2 tourism establishments
- Luque/Limpio restaurants
- Asunción fine dining
- Institutional cafeterias

**Order Characteristics**
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Order Frequency | 1-2x weekly |
| Minimum Order | 10-30 dozen |
| Payment Terms | 7-15 days |
| Preference | Fresh, local |

**Requirements**
- [ ] Fresh delivery (daily preferred)
- [ ] Quality consistency
- [ ] Flexible scheduling
- [ ] Direct relationship

**Pros:**
- Higher margins (15-25%)
- Direct relationships
- Premium product recognition

**Cons:**
- Smaller orders
- Inconsistent demand
- Multiple delivery points

### 2.3 Bakeries & Pastries

**Target Segments**
- Panadería El Dengue
- San Jose bakeries
- Neighborhood panaderías
- Pastelerías

**Order Characteristics**
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Order Frequency | 2-4x weekly |
| Minimum Order | 20-50 dozen |
| Payment Terms | 7-21 days |
| Preference | Large quantities, uniform size |

**Requirements**
- [ ] Bulk quantities
- [ ] Consistent sizing
- [ ] Competitive pricing
- [ ] Reliable supply

**Pros:**
- High volume
- Stable orders
- Lower quality requirements (processing hides defects)

**Cons:**
- Price sensitive
- Quality competition
- Processing-grade acceptable

### 2.4 Wholesalers

**Target Partners**
- Central Market wholesalers
- Distributors in Asunción
- Food service distributors

**Order Characteristics**
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Order Frequency | Daily |
| Minimum Order | 100+ dozen |
| Payment Terms | Cash/7 days |
| Preference | Volume, low price |

**Pros:**
- Large volume
- Quick payment
- Minimal relationship effort

**Cons:**
- Very low margins (3-5%)
- No brand building
- Price competition

### 2.5 Direct Sales

**Models**
1. Farm gate sales
2. Farmers markets
3. Subscription boxes
4. Online ordering with delivery

**Farm Gate**
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Margin | 30-50% |
| Customer | Walk-in, locals |
| Investment | Signage, parking |

**Farmers Markets**
- Teresa Kakuta Market
- Monday market
- Shopping centers

**Pros:**
- Highest margins
- Direct customer feedback
- Brand building
- Flexible pricing

**Cons:**
- Limited volume
- Time intensive
- Weather dependent

### 2.6 Institutions

**Target Segments**
- Schools (public feeding programs)
- Hospitals
- Military bases
- Prisons
- Company cafeterias

**Order Characteristics**
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Order Frequency | Daily/Weekly |
| Volume | Large contracts |
| Payment Terms | 30-60 days |
| Requirements | SENACSA approved, bulk |

**Pros:**
- Large volume contracts
- Stable demand
- Government support

**Cons:**
- Low margins
- Strict requirements
- Long payment cycles
- Political factors

## 3. Channel Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-6)
**Primary Focus:**
1. Bakeries (volume + stable income)
2. Direct sales (high margins)
3. Small restaurants (relationships)

**Target Distribution:**
| Channel | % of Sales |
|---------|------------|
| Bakeries | 40% |
| Direct | 20% |
| Restaurants | 25% |
| Retail | 15% |

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 6-12)
**Add:**
1. Supermarket negotiations
2. Institutional contracts
3. Hotel partnerships

**Target Distribution:**
| Channel | % of Sales |
|---------|------------|
| Bakeries | 35% |
| Supermarkets | 25% |
| Restaurants | 15% |
| Direct | 10% |
| Institutions | 15% |

### Phase 3: Scale (Year 2+)
**Optimize:**
1. Supermarket chains (national)
2. Export preparation
3. Premium channel development

**Target Distribution:**
| Channel | % of Sales |
|---------|------------|
| Supermarkets | 40% |
| Bakeries | 20% |
| Restaurants | 15% |
| Direct/Premium | 15% |
| Institutions | 10% |
