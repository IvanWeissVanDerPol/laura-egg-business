## 6. Feed Mixing Instructions

### Equipment Needed
- Scale (50kg capacity)
- Mixer (horizontal or vertical)
- Buckets/bags for ingredients

### Mixing Procedure

1. **Weigh all ingredients** accurately
2. **Load mixer** with half the grain
3. **Add protein sources**
4. **Add premixes and additives** (mix first with bran)
5. **Add remaining grain**
6. **Mix for 15-20 minutes** (minimum)
7. **Check for uniformity**
8. **Discharge and bag/store**

### Quality Check
- [ ] All ingredients incorporated
- [ ] No visible clumps
- [ ] Consistent color
- [ ] Smells fresh
- [ ] Moisture <13%

## 9. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Possible Cause | Solution |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| Low production | Feed quality | Check protein, energy |
| Thin shells | Calcium low | Increase limestone |
| Poor growth | Protein low | Increase soybean meal |
| Feed refusal | Spoilage | Check moisture, mold |
| Waste | Overfeeding | Reduce daily amount |
| Weight loss | Insufficient feed | Increase ration |
