# Cold Chain Management

## 1. Cold Chain Overview

### Why Cold Chain Matters

| Temperature | Effect on Eggs |
|-------------|----------------|
| 5-8°C | Optimal, 60+ day shelf life |
| 12-15°C | Good, 30-45 day shelf life |
| 20-25°C | Fair, 14-21 day shelf life |
| 30°C+ | Rapid quality loss |

### Quality Degradation by Temperature

| Storage Temp | Haugh Units Lost/Day | Shell Quality |
|--------------|---------------------|---------------|
| 5°C | 0.1 | Excellent |
| 10°C | 0.3 | Good |
| 15°C | 0.5 | Acceptable |
| 25°C | 1.5 | Poor |
| 30°C+ | 3.0 | Unacceptable |

## 2. Temperature Requirements

### Egg Cold Chain Stages

| Stage | Temperature | Humidity | Time Limit |
|-------|-------------|----------|------------|
| On-farm cooling | 15°C max | 70-80% | Within 4 hours |
| Farm storage | 12-15°C | 70-80% | Max 7 days |
| Transport | 8-12°C | 70-80% | Max 8 hours |
| Distribution storage | 8-12°C | 70-80% | Max 3 days |
| Retail display | 8-15°C | 70-80% | Max 7 days |
| Consumer home | Room OK | - | <21 days |

### Temperature Zones

```
[Farm] → [Transport] → [Distribution] → [Retail] → [Consumer]
   ↓
15°C → 12°C → 10°C → 8°C → Room
```

## 5. Transport Cold Chain

### Vehicle Requirements

| Option | Temperature Control | Cost (Gs.) |
|--------|-------------------|------------|
| Refrigerated truck | Yes, 8-12°C | 30,000,000+ |
| Insulated van + ice | 12-18°C | 8,000,000 |
| Insulated van | 15-22°C | 5,000,000 |
| Open truck (early AM) | Ambient | 500,000 |

### Transport Best Practices

1. **Pre-cool eggs** to 12°C before loading
2. **Load in morning** (cooler ambient)
3. **Use ice packs** in non-refrigerated vehicles
4. **Don't mix** with warm products
5. **Maximize load** (full vehicle cools better)
6. **Quick loading/unloading** (minimize door open time)
7. **Direct delivery** (no re-loading points)

## 8. Corrective Actions

### Temperature Excursion Protocol

1. **Document**: Time, duration, temperature
2. **Assess**: Product impact based on duration
3. **Isolate**: Affected product separately
4. **Decide**: Use, sell short-dated, or discard
5. **Investigate**: Find cause
6. **Fix**: Repair equipment, update procedures
7. **Verify**: Confirm fix worked

### Decision Matrix

| Duration Above 15°C | Action |
|--------------------|--------|
| <2 hours | Sell normally, monitor |
| 2-8 hours | Sell within 3 days, inform customer |
| 8-24 hours | Sell within 1 day |
| >24 hours | Evaluate, likely discard |

## 9. Cold Room Maintenance

### Monthly Tasks

- [ ] Clean condenser coils
- [ ] Check door seals
- [ ] Verify drainage
- [ ] Calibrate thermometers
- [ ] Check refrigerant levels
- [ ] Test alarm systems

### Annual Tasks

- [ ] Professional inspection
- [ ] Compressor service
- [ ] Full calibration
- [ ] Insulation check
- [ ] Door replacement if needed

### Troubleshooting

| Problem | Likely Cause | Solution |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Not cooling | Power, compressor | Check power, call technician |
| Fluctuating temp | Door opening, load | Reduce frequency, optimize |
| Ice buildup | Defrost issue | Manual defrost, check settings |
| Warm spots | Air circulation | Rearrange, add fan |
